  • Joel Järvi is a Graphic Designer from Telefonplan outside Stockholm.

    He graduated from the Graphic Design and Illustration (BA) Programme at Konstfack University of Arts, Crafts and Design in spring 2022.

    During his studies at Konstfack and exchange semester at Zhdk in Zurich, Switzerland, he developed a great passion for Layout and Typography.

    Before his studies at Konstfack he dwelled in the realms of technology. He developed tools for Architects and Technical Engineers and designed VR Experiences at the inhouse visualization studio WSP Visual at WSP.

    While studying at Konstfack he also worked at a group home for the mentally and physically injured because he cares a lot about people :)

    In the future he hopes to combine his interest in technology with Layout and Typography while also designing groundbreaking books and caring about people while doing it.

    And...

    He is also very interested in synthesizers, yoga, literature, film, theater, snus, food, kanye west, podcasts etc etc.
