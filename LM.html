  • LM CITY is a book in which I combine non-fiction text with self-produced material about my own experiences of growing up in Telefonplan. It is an attempt to convey a story that is not only about the architectural and cultural-historical non-fiction, but also on an emotional level. Hard facts and soft values. The project was my Bachelor thesis at Graphic Design and Illustration at Konstfack and was exhibited at the spring exhibition in May 2022.
