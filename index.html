    • This website was developed during the summer of 2022 in an attempt to showcase my praxis as a Graphic Designer. For the design of this website I was heavily inspired by the work of Laslo Strong and Dodo at Dwalm, as well as Charlotte Rohde and her Type Design work for the identity of Ornamenta 2024. The ★ was inspired by a piece of Kalevala jewelry. It is a monogram containing the acronym JJ, short for Joel Järvi. In its rotating form my name gets repeated endlessly.The majority of the images used on this website was shot by Vanni Jung Ståhle.
